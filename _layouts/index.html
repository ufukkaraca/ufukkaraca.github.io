Experiences

As a SDG Coordinator for SDSN Youth, I have tackled complex problems on regional and national scales; ranging from climate crisis to gender equality. Having founded the GreenYouth Initiative, I have had the change to work with micromobility firms for building sustainable cities, and educational institutions for creating sustainability frameworks for curriculum-making processes.

Case: GreenYouth Initiative

Founded in 2021, GreenYouth Initiative provides youth-powered sustainability management to private and public institutions alike with services ranging from research and consulting to technological solutions development and execution.

Which means...

I can help you bring sustainability and technology together to create unique solutions, follow the latest trends, oversee large-scale projects and bring a new perspective to your works. I can also help with internal sustainability trainings, reporting and content production.

A sustainable, inclusive and green structure is the backbone of a forward-thinking company, and I can ensure the upholding of these values.

Experience and Education

Studying Electrical Engineering and Management Information Systems majors, I work on turning theory into practice by hands-on experiences and tech-focused projects. I'm a self-taught developer, and I work on developing beautiful things.

Hardware: I have developed an awarded wearable emergency-locating system, small-scale automation circuits and PoC systems for pipelining.

Software: I have designed numerous websites, developed mobile applications and handled the full-stack development for big data, management and HR programs.

Case: Piëch

As a 6-month intern at Piëch Automotive, I developed multiple systems utilizing numerous technologies. The company 'Hub' system, of which I ran the full-stack development, is comprised of over 18,000 code lines.

Including a PLM and HR applications, it enables the company to create real-time AR models from BOM data, in the meanwhile enabling all departments from finance to engineering to work on the most up-to-date data. It also enables real-time control over supplier management, access, and employee vacation management.

Side projects include blockchain prototyping, web parsing and more.

Which means...

I can do front-end, back-end or full-stack development, work on big data management and help manage complex development projects.

Tech Stack
Django, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Python, React, Bootstrap, three.js, Solidity, C, C++, Verilog, Flutter, SQLite.

Organizations

A Turkish Entrepreneurship Foundation Fellow ⓘ , I got the change to learn from some of the best and brightest entrepreneurs in the region. Exclusive workshops, keynotes and more; combined with expansive networking opportunities enabled me to develop my soft skills.

As a result, I got to make the "Roadmap Podcast" with Turkey's leading entrepreneurs, start "YScience" scipreneurship video series with prominent scientists, and attend Spinon entrepreneur incubation program with a foundation scholarship.

Whereas being an intern at Piëch helped me learn working in a start-up environment with a quick pace and wearing multiple hats; being a Microsoft Learn Ambassador helped me develop skills in fast-prototyping, PoC and MVP building and gave me a chance to give-back by organizing workshops.

Case: Roadmap Podcast

Roadmap Podcast, co-hosted with Zeynep Ekin, saw us having C-Level executives from Turkey's biggest start-ups on the other side of the table; with a clear goal of "understanding the entrepreneur's mindset".

One of the very few English podcasts with a focus on Turkish entrepreneurship ecosystem, it included names like Koray Gültekin Bahar, Fevzi Güngör, Sezen Sungur Saral and more. Listen on Spotify.

Which means...

I can work in cross-functional structures and agile environments with a strong understanding of entrepreneurship and project management methodologies, and build a network.
